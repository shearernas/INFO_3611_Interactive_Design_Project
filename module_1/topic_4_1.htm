Topic 4: Installing MySQL on macOS

Follow this link here to bring up the official download page for MySQL from Oracle (the official developers of MySQL and a trusted software company).

You will see 3 dropdown menus towards the left:

  • "Select Version"
  • "Select Operating System"
  • "Select OS Version"

The newest version of MySQL (9.2.0 Innovation) should be selected by default so you can skip the "Select Version" dropdown menu.

Under the "Select Operating System" dropdown menu, select "macOS" from the list of operating systems.

Under the "Select OS Version" dropdown menu, select either "macOS 15 (x86, 64-bit)"if you are installing MySQL on a Mac with an Intel processor, or "macOS 15 (ARM, 64-bit)" if you are installing MySQL on a Mac with an Apple Silicon processor.

If you are unsure of what processor is in your Mac computer, at the menu bar to the top left of your screen, click on the Apple logo, then from the dropdown menu that appears, click on "About This Mac".
